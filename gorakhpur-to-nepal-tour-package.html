Day 1

Gorakhpur Arrival and Lumbini Exploration

Your adventure begins as we pick you up in Gorakhpur for the journey to Pokhara via Lumbini. En route, we explore the UNESCO World Heritage site of Lumbini, the birthplace of Gautam Buddha.

Lumbini highlights: Mayadevi Temple, Ashoka Pillar, World Peace Pagoda, Myanmar Golden Temple, Lumbini Museum, and China Temple.

Travel Info: 350 km via Lumbini, 9 hours

Overnight: Hotel in Pokhara

Day 2

Full-Day Pokhara Sightseeing

After breakfast, we explore the captivating sights of Pokhara, known as the "Tourist Capital of Nepal." Our Pokhara tour includes Devi’s fall, Gupteshwar cave, Bindhyabasini temple, Seti River, Mahendra cave, Bat cave, and Barahi temple. You can enjoy a serene boat ride on Phewa Lake and explore Lakeside at your own pace.

Overnight: Hotel in Pokhara

Day 3

Journey to Kathmandu via Manakamna Temple

After an early breakfast, we set out for Kathmandu via the sacred Manakamna Temple. This temple, dedicated to the Hindu Goddess Bhagwati, is known for granting the wishes of devotees. The temple offers breathtaking views of deep valleys and the Himalayan mountains.

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu

Day 4

Full-Day Kathmandu Exploration

Following breakfast, we embark on a comprehensive tour of Kathmandu's cultural gems. Our itinerary includes Pashupatinath Temple, Goheshwari Devi temple, Bouddhanath Stupa, Jalnarayan temple, and Bhaktapur or Patan Darbar Square. Optional: Experience the majesty of Mount Everest with an early morning mountain flight.

Overnight: Hotel in Kathmandu

Day 5

Return to Gorakhpur

After breakfast, we embark on a scenic 10-hour journey back to Gorakhpur. We part ways with cherished memories and hope to welcome you on another adventure in the future.

Travel Info: 333 km via Narayanghat, Bharatpur (Very Safe Road)

How to Reach Gorakhpur for Your Nepal Tour Package

How to Reach Gorakhpur by Rail

Gorakhpur boasts excellent rail connectivity to all corners of India. Serving as the headquarters of the North Eastern Railway, this rail hub links you to major cities nationwide. You can easily access Gorakhpur from cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Chennai, Nagpur, Varanasi, Lucknow, and more.

How to Reach Gorakhpur by Air

The Gorakhpur Civil Airport, located approximately 8 kilometers from the city center, is your nearest air gateway. This airport, officially known as Mahayogi Gorakhnath Airport, offers direct flights to key destinations including Delhi, Varanasi, Lucknow, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Allahabad, Kolkata, and others. While it primarily caters to Indian Air Force traffic, it also operates civil aviation services.

How to Reach Gorakhpur by Road

A network of buses provides regular services to various cities, including Kushinagar (50 km), Varanasi (235 km), Lucknow (276 km), and Allahabad (339 km). Both state-owned and private buses are available for your journey. You can find direct buses from Gorakhpur to Lucknow and other cities with ease. Travel to Gorakhpur by road is a convenient option.
